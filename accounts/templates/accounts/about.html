Learn More About Omar

I'm Omar El Mahdy, a coach and nutrition specialist, and I've been focused on female training for over 4 years. During that time, I've trained more than 1,000 women with different body types and goals.

I work with women who struggle with eating disorders, IBS, insulin resistance, PCOS, thyroid issues (whether overactive or underactive), as well as those with high blood pressure, type 1 and type 2 diabetes, diastasis recti, post-bariatric surgery cases, pregnant women, and breastfeeding mothers.

I work alongside a full medical team that includes a physiotherapist, a pharmacist, and a physician — to make sure every girl gets the best possible support throughout her health journey.

I hold more than 10 certificates and diplomas in nutrition, training, mental and physical health, eating disorders, and psychology.

I'm specialized in female hormones, and my entire work revolves around improving women’s shape, strength, and health — both physically and mentally — while also changing how they see themselves and their relationship with food, so they can become stronger and better in their daily lives. I've helped many women achieve amazing results, and my goal is to help every girl become the best version of herself.

Frequently Asked Questions About Online Coaching

What is a training plan without follow-up?

After payment, you’ll receive a complete training plan that includes sets, reps, rest time, my personal notes for each workout, demonstration videos, and alternatives for exercises if needed—so it feels like I’m right there with you at the gym. You’ll also get guideline documents that tell you exactly what to do from the moment you walk into the gym until you leave. It’s a one-time plan that you can keep for life.

Who is this training plan suitable for?

It’s perfect for any woman who wants to tone her body and reach her fitness goals. However, it’s not suitable for pregnant women or anyone with specific injuries—those should consult their doctor or subscribe to my full coaching packages that include doctor follow-up.

Can beginners follow these training plans?

Absolutely! Every plan is designed with beginners in mind. I break down exercise performance from scratch and provide variations to suit different fitness levels. Whether you're new to training or have 10–15 years of experience, these plans will work for you.

Can I follow the plan at home or only at the gym?

You can absolutely follow it at home or at the gym! Each plan includes home-friendly alternatives, clear video demos, reps, weights, and rest times to make training accessible no matter your location.

How do I receive my training plan?

Once payment is complete, you’ll be redirected to a page where your training plan is instantly available and ready to use.

Why should I, as a woman, buy this training plan?

In short: I've coached over 1,500 women in the past 4 years and helped them achieve incredible results. I'm a specialist in female hormones, training, and nutrition. I hold multiple diplomas and certifications that focus on women’s health. My plans are designed to support hormonal balance, muscle tone, mental well-being, and build a strong, confident relationship with food and training.
